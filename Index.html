About Bruno Ortuno:

Hello! My name is Bruno Ortuno, I'm 32 years old and I live in Buenos Aires, Argentina by the moment. I'm a Fullstack developer, with a primary focus on Backend.

I self-learned english since I was 12 years old, so I can easily read and write in english, despite it not being my mother tongue. And I can maintain conversations in english, up to a point, since I don't get the chance to practice that much. I also got my N6 Nihongo Tokimeki at Japanese!

Off the programming aspect, I did study for quite some time Psichology as well as Physics in college. (yeah, quite a mix) During the pandemic I learned the basics about technical analysis of stock market investment.

I guess that's enough about me! But feel free to ask whatever you feel curious about me and write me through any via you feel fit, that I will answer as soon as I can!
